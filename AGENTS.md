# AGENTS.md — NEIJING ATLAS

This project follows `tutu-manage-project` conventions.

## Product boundary

NEIJING ATLAS is a traditional body-pattern tracking and reflection framework inspired by the Huangdi Neijing and classical Chinese medicine concepts.

It is **not** a diagnosis, treatment, prescription, or emergency-triage product.

## Safety rules

- Never claim the system diagnoses a disease or identifies a TCM syndrome as medical fact.
- Use language like “pattern lens”, “relevance”, “signals”, “reflection”, and “observation”.
- Avoid herbal prescriptions, acupuncture instructions, medical treatment plans, or “you have X deficiency/excess”.
- Always include red-flag routing for urgent/severe symptoms.
- Source-ground classical concepts; preserve uncertainty and reviewer notes.
