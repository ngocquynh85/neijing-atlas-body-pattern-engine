## Product thesis

Modern health trackers collect isolated metrics. NEIJING ATLAS explores a pattern-first framework inspired by classical Chinese medicine: observing relationships between sleep, food, emotion, temperature, moisture, season, work rhythm, and body sensation.

## Non-goals

- medical diagnosis
- herbal prescription
- treatment advice
- replacing clinicians
